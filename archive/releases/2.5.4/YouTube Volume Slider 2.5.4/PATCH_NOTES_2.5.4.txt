## Changed

- Replaced the speaker icon inside the circular volume arc with a compact numeric volume indicator.
- Removed the percent sign from the circular indicator so values have more room and remain easier to read.
- Increased the circular indicator number to match the native duration text size, including a fitted 14px treatment for `100`.
- Centered the circular indicator text, thinned the progress ring, and tightened collapsed clipping so the slider thumb stays hidden.
- Kept the slider row at its full expanded width during open and close animation so the 100% thumb no longer travels beside the arc.
- Nudged the volume bar closer to the arc and widened the ring radius to give the number more interior breathing room.
- Removed the zero-volume arc seam and optically aligned the compact `100` label with the two-digit values.
- Displayed muted volume as `M` inside the arc while keeping the accessible label as "Muted".
- Added mouse-wheel volume control on the circular mute/volume indicator, changing volume in 5-point steps.
- Moved the old visible percentage label out of the layout so the slider bar sits closer to the arc.
