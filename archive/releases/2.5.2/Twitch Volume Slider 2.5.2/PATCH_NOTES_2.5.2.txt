# Volume Sliders 2.5.2

## Changed

- Changes the default Bar thickness setting from 50% to 75%.
- Scales the slider grabber with the selected bar thickness so it stays visually balanced at thinner and thicker settings.
- Keeps grabber size changes in sync with the bar thickness preview instead of easing slightly behind it.
- Expands the volume slider while the Bar thickness setting is being dragged, then restores the normal collapsed state when Always expanded is off.
- Previews on-video opacity settings while dragging: Idle shows the collapsed slider and Active shows the expanded slider.
- Restores the Show on video expand/collapse animation to the centered version 2.5 behavior.
- Fixes the first settings-slider hold after video controls hide and reappear so the volume slider expands immediately instead of only on later drags.
